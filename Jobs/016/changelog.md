🛠️ Dev Notes — Crash-site start & rope launch

🪂 You now start at the CRASH SITE, not already on the boat — the plane went down by the river.
⛵ The boat waits MOORED at the water's edge, tied up with a rope. It won't move until you launch.
👥 A banner shows how many of the crew are aboard — "2/5 on boat" — so nobody gets left behind.
🪢 When you're ready, UNTIE THE ROPE to launch — the boat is freed and the run begins.
🐊 No danger until you cast off: enemies only start once the rope is untied. Gear up first!
🎨 The crash-site area is a rough greybox for now — a proper hand-built hub comes later.
